How To Contribute?

Step 1: Obtain Gazette Footage

Where can you find them?

• You can find them on Youtube and advanced YouTube search tools.

• You can find them in old Growtopia Forums posts.

• You can check your gallery for old photos/videos.

Step 2: Check Footages

Check footages and be sure they are not fake or from a private server.

Step 3: Send Us

Where you can contact us?

• Send us E-mail.

• Join our Discord server.

Also give us information about footage like date, source etc.
